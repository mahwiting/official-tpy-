About Thokoza Progressive Youth

Founded in 2010, Thokoza Progressive Youth is a non-profit organization committed to youth development and community empowerment in Thokoza and surrounding areas.

Our mission is to create platforms for young people to develop their potential, acquire skills, and become active contributors to society.
