Our Programs

Discover the various initiatives we offer to empower and uplift the youth in our community.

Our Offerings

After-School Tutoring

Mon-Fri, 3pm-6pm Tsepo Street Center Grades 8-12

Free tutoring in mathematics, science, and languages for high school students to improve academic performance.

Learn More

Career Guidance

Monthly Workshops Various Locations Grades 10-12

Workshops and one-on-one sessions to help students make informed career choices and prepare for higher education.

Learn More

Computer Skills Training

Tues & Thurs, 4pm-6pm Computer Lab Ages 16+

Basic to intermediate computer literacy courses including Microsoft Office, internet skills, and basic coding.

Learn More

Entrepreneurship Program

Sat, 9am-12pm Tsepo Street Center Ages 18-35

Training and mentorship for aspiring entrepreneurs, including business planning and financial management.

Learn More

Soccer Development

Wed & Sat, 2pm-5pm Local Sports Field Ages 10-18

Youth soccer program promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and discipline through structured training.

Learn More

Creative Arts

Fri, 3pm-6pm Arts Center All Ages

Workshops in visual arts, drama, and music to foster creativity and self-expression among youth.

Learn More

Youth Mentorship

Flexible Schedule Various Locations Ages 14-21

One-on-one mentorship pairing youth with positive role models for guidance and personal development.

Learn More

Community Outreach

Monthly Events Thokoza Community All Ages

Initiatives addressing community needs including health awareness, clean-ups, and social support programs.
