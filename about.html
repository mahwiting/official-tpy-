About Our Organization

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team behind Thokoza Progressive Youth.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower young people in Thokoza through education, skills development, and community engagement, creating opportunities for personal growth and positive social change.

Vision

A community where every young person has access to opportunities that enable them to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Values

  • Empowerment
  • Integrity
  • Community
  • Innovation
  • Accountability

Our History

2010

Foundation

Thokoza Progressive Youth was founded by a group of community activists concerned about youth unemployment and lack of opportunities in the area.

2012

First Programs

Launched our first after-school tutoring program and sports development initiative with 50 participants.

2015

Community Recognition

Received recognition from local government for our contribution to youth development in Thokoza.

2018

Expansion

Expanded our programs to include vocational training and established partnerships with local businesses.

2025

Today

Now serving over 500 youth annually with 12 different programs and a team of dedicated staff and volunteers.
